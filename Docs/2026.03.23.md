## 🔬 Special Research Points

### 🎯 Research Point A: The "Context Gap" in Industrial Schematics
Traditional RAG treats images as supplemental tags appended to text. In industrial environments, this causes a "context gap" during high-stress repairs. 
- **Finding**: By forcing the LLM to **interleave** technical diagrams within the instruction flow (using tag-injection), we observed a significant improvement in operator task-completion speed (simulated). 
- **Result**: Visuals are no longer "Supplemental Records"—they are **Inline Evidence**.

### 🖼️ Research Point B: VLM-Based Captioning vs. OCR
Standard OCR fails on complex sensor maps (e.g., T-101 vs. V-101 labels).
- **Finding**: Using **GPT-4o Vision** for "contextual captioning" during ingestion allows the system to index images based on *intent* rather than just *coordinates*.
- **Impact**: Searching for "impeller maintenance" now retrieves the exact diagram showing the impeller clearance, even if the text nearby is sparse.

### 🔗 Research Point C: Universal Path Standardization
Industrial digital twins often run on mixed environments (Windows for UI, Linux for GPU-heavy inference).
- **Finding**: Asset resolution (404s) is the #1 killer of multimodal UX. We implemented an absolute-path static mount with universal forward-slash normalization across the database, RAG pipeline, and React frontend.
- **Infrastructure**: Transitioning to `127.0.0.1` standardized on port 8000 for local network parity.
